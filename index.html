Changes we made

At a global level, we've identified four focus areas for our Equity, Diversity and Inclusion strategy: gender, race and ethnicity, people with disabilities, and LGBTQI+ communities. We're working to address the challenges of these groups' under-representation at all levels of our organisation. However, we know that under-representation is not confined to people in these groups alone – so our approach supports each of our markets in identifying who is under-represented or excluded, and why.
